# Validation

Cross-checks of ALADYN against analytical solutions and published benchmarks.

- `analytical/` — closed-form sanity checks (e.g. spherical pendulum period,
  rigid-body free precession, energy conservation).
- `benchmarks/` — reproduction of standard multibody benchmarks (IFToMM
  spatial benchmarks, ADAMS / MBDyn comparison cases).

Each subfolder owns its own README, models, results and report.
